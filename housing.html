Mission Statement:

We are committed to empowering South Asian women in Southern Michigan by providing safe housing resources and support for those facing domestic violence. Through culturally sensitive assistance and trusted connections, we strive to create pathways to safety, stability, and independence.

What resources do we provide at the shelters?

  • -Emergency shelter and crisis housing
  • -Rent assistance and eviction prevention
  • -Help finding long-term housing
  • -Support for applying to subsidized housing
  • -Referrals to transitional or supportive housing
  • -Assistance for newcomers and refugees
  • -Landlord mediation and advocacy
  • -Case management and housing navigation
