MAI Family Services Counseling

MAI's Mission Statement:

Mai Family Services seeks to raise awareness of the problems facing the South Asian community in Michigan and to provide support services in the areas of partner and family violence, substance abuse, mental health and senior issues through confidential and culturally sensitive intervention.

Are you in need of counseling services?

Well, you've come to the right spot. At MAI Family Services, Counseling is a large part of our service to our community. We encourage people to reach out for help.

Here at MAI, we focus on prevention and intervention. Below are the services we provide.

For provide counseling in the following areas:

  1. Abuse
  2. Prevention
  3. Grief and bereavement support
  4. Issues affecting seniors
  5. Substance abuse
  6. Adjustment to new culture
  7. Marriage Counselling
  8. Toll free and confidential helpline and support in multiple Indian languages
  9. Referrals to shelters, attorneys, community health resources, therapists
  10. Awareness training workshops for community members
  11. Effective Communication
  12. Workplace Challenge
  13. Mental Health issues
