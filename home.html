Desi Roots

Mission Statement

At Desi Roots, we are dedicated to empowering South Asian women in Southern Michigan who are experiencing domestic violence. By offering culturally sensitive counseling, multilingual hotlines, and access to housing shelter resources, we provide compassionate, informed support that understands the unique challenges faced by our community. Manavi: A Multilingual Hotline

Founded in 1985, Manavi is the first organization in the United States committed to ending all forms of violence against South Asian survivors. With a strong survivor-centered approach, Manavi offers culturally specific and linguistically appropriate services tailored to the unique needs of the South Asian community. Our dedicated staff and volunteers, fluent in over 14 South Asian languages, work tirelessly to support survivors and build a violence-free future.

MAI Family Services: Counseling and more!

Mai Family Services supports Michigan's South Asian community by raising awareness and providing confidential, culturally sensitive assistance for issues like family violence, substance abuse, mental health, and senior care.

Housing Options

We are committed to empowering South Asian women in Southern Michigan by providing safe housing resources and support for those facing domestic violence. Through culturally sensitive assistance and trusted connections, we strive to create pathways to safety, stability, and independence.
